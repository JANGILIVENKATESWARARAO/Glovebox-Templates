What is the
Motability?
The Motability Scheme enables eligible people with a disability to lease a
brand-new car, scooter or powered wheelchair by exchanging their
qualifying mobility allowance. With over 600,000 customers across the
UK, the scheme helps people stay mobile and independent, stress-free.
