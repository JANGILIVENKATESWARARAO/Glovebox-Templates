What’s Included?

When you lease through the Motability Scheme, everything you need to keep driving is taken care of — it’s all included in the price.

Insurance cover

We’ll arrange this for you. You can add up to three drivers and can change these at any time.

Servicing and MOTs

We’ll cover your regular services and routine repairs, to keep your vehicle running perfectly.

Breakdown cover

We’ll give you breakdown cover so you can travel with peace of mind. If you get a car or a WAV/ Wheelchair Accessible Vehicle, you’ll get RAC cover.

Support with your EV

We’ll arrange a home chargepoint and a standard installation as part of your lease, or give you access to the bp pulse network of over 9,000 public chargepoints. This depends on a few things.
